Homework 12: GEE Analysis Pt2

Instructions: Calculate the NDVI on a Farm, Forest and an Urban Area

Search for an agricultural landscape in some other place of the world besides the USA. Make three polygons that are less than 1 mile apart and that are at least one hectare in area. One polygon should be covering a farm, another a forest and another an urban area. Calculate the mean, max and min NDVI for each of the polygons. Share your GEE code.
